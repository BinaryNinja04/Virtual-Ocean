Octopus:
Scientific name: Octopoda
Breed: Cephalopoda.
Feed on: crabs, clams, snails, small fishes, and even other octopuses
Found At: 1,000 m (3,300 ft)
Some amazing facts
1. Octopus arms have a mind of their own.
2. They have (literally) a lot of heart.
3. Males die after mating, too.
4. They are masters of camouflage.
5. They are smart enough to use tools.
6. They can regrow limbs.
7. lifespan is around 1 to 5 years.
