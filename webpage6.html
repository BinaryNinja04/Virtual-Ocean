Scientific Name: Balaenoptera musculus (Blue Whale)
Breed: Whales are mammals, and there are numerous species.
Feeds On: Whales are filter feeders, with diet variations depending on the species. Blue Whales, for example, primarily feed on krill.
Found at Depth: Whales can be found at different depths in the ocean, with some species diving to great depths while others prefer shallower waters.
Two Facts:
1. Blue Whales are the largest animals on Earth, with tongues alone weighing as much as an elephant.
2. Whales are known for their complex songs, which are used for communication and can travel great distances underwater.
