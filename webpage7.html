Scientific Name:Loligo vulgaris (Common Squid)
Breed:Squid are cephalopods, and there are many species.
Feeds On: Squid are carnivorous and prey on small fish and crustaceans.
Found at Depth:Squid can be found at various depths in the ocean, ranging from near the surface to deep-sea environments.
Two Facts:
1. Squid have specialized jet-propulsion systems called siphons that allow them to move rapidly through the water.
2. Some species of squid, like the giant squid, are known for their enormous size and elusive nature, making them subjects of fascination and mystery.
