RED FISH:
Scientific name: Sciaenops Ocellatus
Breed: The red drum ( Sciaenops Ocellatus ), also known as channel bass, red fish, spottail base or simply red, is a game fish.
Feed on: Mullet, Croaker, Shrimp
Found At: Upto 100ft deep
Some amazing facts
Redfish can grow up to 40 inches in length.
Redfish are opportunistic feeders
Redfish are known for their powerful fighting ability.
Redfish change colour depending on their environment.
The life span of a red span can exceed 40 years
