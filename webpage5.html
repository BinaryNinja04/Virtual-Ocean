Scientific Name: Carcharodon carcharias (Great White Shark)
Breed: Sharks are a diverse group with many species.
Feeds On: Sharks are carnivorous and primarily feed on fish, seals, and other marine animals.
Found at Depth: Sharks can be found at various depths in the ocean, depending on the species, ranging from shallow coastal waters to deep-sea environments.
Two Facts
1. Sharks have a highly developed sense of smell, helping them locate prey from long distances.
2. Some shark species, like the Greenland Shark, are known to have incredibly long lifespans, possibly reaching several hundred years.
